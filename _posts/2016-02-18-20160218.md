## angularjs 的常用功能

* 迭代输出：ng-repeat
* 数据绑定：ng-model, 双大括号
* 点击事件：ng-click, ng-dblclick
* 条件判断：ng-switch on, ng-switch-when, ng-if, ng-show, ng-hide, ng-disabled
* 表单验证：ng-trim, ng-minlength, ng-maxlength, required, ng-pattern
* 下拉框：ng-options
* 样式：ng-style
* ajax：$http
